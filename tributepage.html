S.P.BALA SUBRAHMANYAM

VOICE OF MUSIC

BIOGRAPHY

Sripathi Panditaradhyula Balasubrahmanyam .He is widely regarded as one of the greatest Indian singers of all time. balasubrahmanyam garu debuted as a playback singer on 15 December 1966. He held the Guinness World Record for recording the highest number of songs by a singer with over 50,000 songs in 16 languages.
